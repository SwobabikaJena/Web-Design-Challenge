Max Temperature

The above plot analyses the relationship between a city's latitude with its maximum observed temperature. The plot shows a negative association about the equator (0-degree lat) i.e. as we move away from the equator, both towards north pole (+ 90 degree lat) and south pole (- 90 degree lat), the maximum temperature starts decreasing with increase in latitude position.
