Wind Speed

The above plot displays the relationship between a city's latitude with its wind speed (in mph). The plot does not show any particular trend and hence it can be concluded that, the change in latitude position has no effect on the city's wind speed. However, the wind speeds in majority of the cities lie in the lower half of the graph indicating low wind speeds irrespective of the latitude position.
